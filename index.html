birthday bread

Birthday Bread is a goodwill project created by James Stewart. The mission of the Birthday Bread project is to spread joy via bread. The creator does not retain any profits and the only costs associated with this service are to ship a delicious loaf of bread to the doorstep of your loved one. Here is an sample of what to expect when it arrives.

The process is simple:

  1. Text "bread" to the bread man (541) 241-6749.
  2. Bread man bakes.
  3. Your loved one receives a fresh, nutritious, wholesome, delicious loaf of bread.
